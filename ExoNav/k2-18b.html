K2-18 b

Potentially Earth-like

k2-18 b orbits a star similar to our Sun and has a chance of harboring life. Scientists believe its conditions could be suitable for life as we know it, making it a prime candidate for further exploration.

  • Mass: 5 times that of Earth
  • Radius: 1.6 times that of Earth
  • Surface Gravity: 2g
  • Orbital Period: 385 days
